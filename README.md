# 🏋️ HEFT – Workout Tracker

A mobile-first workout tracker for logging weight training sessions, building routines, and tracking strength progress over time.

---

## 📋 Log Tab

- Use the **‹ ›** arrows to navigate between days
- Tap **+ Add Exercise** to add a single exercise to today's session
- Tap **+ Load Routine** to load a pre-built routine (appears once you have routines saved)
- For each exercise, log your sets with **weight (kg)** and **reps**
- Tap **+ Add Set** to add more sets, or **−** to remove one
- The previous session's data is shown as a hint below the column headers
- A **🏆 PB** badge appears automatically when you hit a new personal best weight
- Tap **✕** on an exercise block to remove it from the day's log

---

## 🔁 Routines Tab

- Tap **+ New** to create a routine
- Give it a name (e.g. Push Day, Pull Day, Legs) and select exercises from the library
- Tap **Edit** to update a routine or **✕** to delete it
- When loading a routine from the Log tab, each exercise shows the **top weight × reps** from your last session so you know where to pick up

---

## 💪 Exercises Tab

- Browse the built-in library of 15 exercises, filterable by muscle group
- Use the search bar to find exercises quickly
- Tap **+ New** to add a custom exercise — give it a name and assign a muscle group
- Custom exercises can be deleted with **✕**; built-in exercises cannot be removed

---

## 📈 Progress Tab

- Only exercises you have logged at least once will appear here
- Select an exercise from the dropdown to view its trend
- Switch between three metrics:
  - **Max Weight** — heaviest set logged per session (kg)
  - **Volume** — total weight × reps across all sets per session
  - **Max Reps** — highest rep count in a single set per session
- The chart shows the last **90 days** of data
- Stats cards show your **peak**, **latest**, and **% change** over 90 days
- A full session history list is shown below the chart

---

## 💾 Data & Storage

- All data is saved locally on your device using browser storage
- No account or internet connection required after the app loads
- Data is tied to the browser/device — clearing browser data will erase your workout history
- To back up your data, use your browser's export tools or consider copying the localStorage values periodically

---

## 📱 Installing on Your Phone

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the app URL in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add** — the app opens fullscreen like a native app

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app URL in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add**
